Hello dear Sweet Lover!

Welcome to this sweet community!

My name is Andra-Diana POPESCU and I am passionate about desserts.

The "Iubitorii de dulce" brand was founded on May 2, 2020, during the Coronavirus pandemic (COVID-19). The free time in lockdown allowed me to develop my creativity in the field of confectionery. On September 7, 2020, I graduated the professional training course in making confectionery products.

In Romanian, "Iubitorii de dulce" means "Lovers of sweets" or "people who love sweets". The ingredients used are of superior quality, most being imported from Italy.

Food allergy notice: Please be advised that the food prepared may contain milk, eggs, wheat, soy, peanuts or tree nuts. Some items are cooked to order and may be served raw or undercooked. Consuming these products may increase the risk of foodborne illness.
